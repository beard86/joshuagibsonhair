One of London’s leading hair stylists, Joshua Gibson graduated with distinction from the renowned hair brand Sassoon in 1993 and began his career in hair at the Manchester salon as a barber and stylist. He has since worked on stages across the world from Toronto to Tokyo.

Today Josh continues to teach at the Vidal Sassoon academy but is also a leading men’s editorial stylist working with photographers like Walter Pfeiffer, Daniel Reira and Li Qi.

Josh’s work has appeared in GQ Style, L’Uomo Vogue, i-D and Fantastic Man magazines. Josh is also in demand as a commentator on hair and fashion trends contributing to the best-selling style guide 'How to be Adored' by Caroline Cox, The Sunday Times Style magazine, Times Online, The Economist and Radio 4 and he features in 'London Lives' a book of the city’s creatives.

His collaborations with London designers Agi & Sam, Nicole Farhi and YMC achieved much acclaim and column inches and his personal style has been recognised with a series of modelling assignments for fashion editorial shot by Nick Knight, Daniel Reira and Andreas Larsson.
